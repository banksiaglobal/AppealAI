About Us

Banksia Global is an international boutique consultancy headquartered in Sydney, Australia, providing professional services for InterSystems technologies. At Banksia Global we deliver full-cycle software development services to mid-size companies and large enterprises. Our team members have decades of experience building innovative solutions on InterSystems platforms.

Our Services

  • Development: Full-Cycle Development services from a single module to complex software systems, data migration projects, cloud migration, IRIS upgrades
  • Integration: Building interoperability between systems
  • IRIS Adoption Accelerator: Our team will work with your specialists to help you set up for success in IRIS adoption. We will bring and help you to adopt proven techniques in solution architecture, change control, continuous integration/continuous delivery, automated testing, deployment, upgrades and support. We are also an authorised training partner of InterSystems.
  • Consulting: We have real-world experience and provide comprehensive consulting services for the whole range of InterSystems products: IRIS, IRIS for Health, HealthShare Health Connect and TrakCare. We will help you find the best solution for your needs.
